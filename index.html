Microsoft Digital Trust & Security Summit: Paris - AI Tour Pre-Day

Where: Brazil

When: Tuesday, March 10, 2026

Join us for an exclusive pre-day event focused on securing the AI era through digital trust. Explore how Microsoft is building trust through security, compliance, and responsible AI, and learn how Brazil organizations are balancing rapid AI innovation with NIS2, DORA, GDPR, and AI Act requirements.

Event Agenda

Join us for an exclusive pre-day event focused on securing the AI era through digital trust. Explore how Microsoft is building trust through security, compliance, and responsible AI, and learn how Brazil organizations are balancing rapid AI innovation with NIS2, DORA, GDPR, and AI Act requirements.

The agenda will explore:

  • Trust as the Foundation for Digital Growth – a global perspective on how regulatory frameworks are becoming more stringent alongside the rapid shifts in AI, cybersecurity, privacy, and sovereignty, and what this means for organizations operating in Brazil.
  • From Regulation to Resilience – how Brazil organizations are balancing rapid AI innovation with NIS2, DORA, GDPR, and AI Act requirements.
  • Security & Regulatory Case Studies – building trusted AI in Brazil across FSI, Public Sector, and Healthcare sectors.
  • Microsoft Security & Compliance Showcase – building a secure, trusted AI platform with Purview, Entra ID, Defender, and Sentinel.
  • Interactive Roundtables – building your security & regulatory readiness blueprint for 2026.
