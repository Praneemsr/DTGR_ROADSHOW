Microsoft Digital Trust & Security Summit: Paris

Exclusive Pre-Day Event for the Microsoft AI Tour

Hosted by Microsoft Digital Trust & Global Regulations Program

Where: Paris, France

When: Tuesday, March 10, 2026, 9:00 AM – 3:15 PM CET

Welcome to a Transformative Dialogue on Digital Trust

In an era where artificial intelligence is reshaping industries at unprecedented speed, building and maintaining digital trust has never been more critical. We invite you to join us for this exclusive, invitation-only pre-day summit on March 10, 2026—the day before the Microsoft AI Tour Paris begins on March 11.

This meticulously curated event convenes Europe's leading experts in security, compliance, privacy, and regulatory affairs. Together, we will explore how organizations can navigate evolving regulatory landscapes while fully harnessing responsible AI and cloud technologies to drive innovation, resilience, and growth. Key focus areas include operationalizing the EU AI Act, achieving DORA operational resilience, strengthening defenses under NIS2, ensuring GDPR compliance, and safeguarding data sovereignty. This summit delivers forward-looking, practical strategies customized to the French and broader European context—transforming regulatory obligations into competitive advantages that foster enduring trust with customers, partners, and stakeholders.

Why This Summit Is Essential for Your Organization

In today's complex regulatory environment, compliance has evolved from a baseline requirement to a powerful strategic differentiator. This summit equips you with the knowledge, tools, and connections to lead with confidence:

  • Unparalleled Exclusivity — Capped at 120 attendees to foster deep, meaningful conversations and direct access to Microsoft experts for personalized 1:1 discussions.
  • Deep Regulatory Expertise — Acquire practical clarity on implementing NIS2, DORA, EU AI Act, GDPR, and data sovereignty mandates in real-world operations.
  • Actionable, Personalized Outcomes — Depart with a co-created 90-day readiness plan and a detailed 12–18 month strategic roadmap tailored to your organization's priorities.
  • Hands-On Solution Exposure — Engage with live demonstrations of Microsoft's integrated platforms—including Purview, Entra ID, Defender XDR, Sentinel, and AI Assurance—directly mapped to compliance and security challenges.
  • High-Value Peer Networking — Build lasting relationships with fellow leaders navigating similar issues, sparking collaborations that continue well beyond the event.
  • Strategic Timing & Continuity — Gain immediate momentum for the Microsoft AI Tour on March 11, with customized guidance on relevant security, compliance, and AI governance sessions.
