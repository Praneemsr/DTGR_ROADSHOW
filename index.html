Microsoft Digital Trust & Global Regulations Program

Microsoft Digital Trust & Security Summit: Paris - AI Tour Pre-Day

Where: Paris, France

When: Tuesday, March 10, 2026

Audience: Up to 120 attendees (C-suite, CISOs, DPOs, regulators, security & compliance leaders)

Join us for an exclusive pre-day event focused on securing the AI era through digital trust. Explore how Microsoft is building trust through security, compliance, and responsible AI, and learn how French organizations are balancing rapid AI innovation with NIS2, DORA, GDPR, and EU AI Act requirements.

Event Agenda

8:30 AM – 9:00 AM

Registration

Arrival, registration. Background loop with short videos on Microsoft Security, Digital Trust, and AI Tour.

9:00 AM – 9:45 AM

KEYNOTE: Securing the AI Era: Digital Trust as a Growth Engine for France

Delivered by a Corporate CELA leader, French NTO/NSO, or Security CVP

  • Framing the European and French landscape (AI, cybersecurity, privacy, sovereignty, NIS2, DORA, EU AI Act)
  • Microsoft's commitment: building trust through security, compliance, and responsible AI, not just technology
  • Call to action: use Microsoft as a strategic partner for secure, compliant AI transformation
9:45 AM – 10:30 AM

FIRESIDE CHAT: From Regulation to Resilience: How French Organizations Secure AI and Cloud

Moderated by a CELA or Security leader with participants from French CISOs, regulators, and industry experts

  • How organizations in France are balancing rapid AI innovation with NIS2, DORA, GDPR, and EU AI Act requirements
  • Examples of AI and cyber audits, lawful access requests, and cross-border data considerations in the French/EU context
  • How Microsoft operationalizes risk management through product design, security operations, and customer support
  • AI governance and Responsible AI as differentiators for security and compliance
10:30 AM – 10:45 AM

Break

Coffee break and informal networking with Microsoft experts

10:45 AM – 12:15 PM

BREAKOUT SESSION: Security & Regulatory Case Studies – Building Trusted AI in France and the EU

  • FSI: Adapting to DORA, NIS2, and EU AI Act while modernizing payments, fraud detection, and customer experience
  • Public Sector & Sovereignty: Data residency, lawful access, and the EU Data Boundary for French government and regulated workloads
  • Healthcare & Life Sciences: Protecting sensitive health data, clinical AI use cases, and cross-border research collaboration under GDPR

Takeaway: A playbook of real-world security and compliance patterns, including architecture diagrams and references to local French success stories

12:15 PM – 1:15 PM

Lunch

Networking lunch with dedicated tables by industry (FSI, PS, HLS, MFG, RCG)

1:15 PM – 2:15 PM

BREAKOUT SESSION: Microsoft Security & Compliance Showcase: Building a Secure, Trusted AI Platform

  • Microsoft Purview Compliance Manager & Regulatory Navigator: Mapping EU & French requirements (NIS2, DORA, EU AI Act, GDPR) to actionable controls and assessments
  • DSPM for AI / Data Security Posture Management: Visibility and control over sensitive data used by AI workloads
  • Entra ID & Identity Security: Zero Trust, Conditional Access, Privileged Identity Management, Verified ID
  • Defender XDR & Sentinel: Unified threat protection, incident response, and AI-powered SOC capabilities
  • Information Protection & DLP: Classifying, labeling, and protecting sensitive data across Microsoft 365, Azure, and multi-cloud environments

Takeaway: Clear view of how Microsoft Security and Purview work together to secure AI, protect data, and demonstrate compliance

2:15 PM – 2:30 PM

Break

Coffee break and transition to interactive sessions

2:30 PM – 3:45 PM

Interactive Roundtables: Building Your Security & Regulatory Readiness Blueprint for 2025

  • Breakouts by industry and/or role (CISO, DPO, Risk/Compliance, Architect)
  • Each group identifies its top 3–5 security and regulatory priorities (e.g., NIS2 readiness, DORA operational resilience, EU AI Act governance, identity modernization, SOC transformation)
  • Mapping these priorities to Microsoft capabilities (Purview, Entra, Defender, Sentinel, Azure, M365) and partner services

Outcome: Each participant leaves with a 90-day readiness plan and a high-level roadmap for the next 12–18 months

3:45 PM – 4:15 PM

Closing & Networking: From Paris to the AI Tour – Next Steps

  • Short closing by Microsoft host summarizing key takeaways and how they connect to the AI Tour main day on March 11
  • Invitation to specific AI Tour sessions (security, compliance, and AI governance tracks)
  • Open networking and 1:1 meetings with Microsoft, CELA, and partner experts
